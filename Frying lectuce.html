Frying Vegies

Ingredients:

Any types of vegetables you like

A bit of salt

Garlic (option)

Steps:

1. If your choice of vegetables requires cutting, rinse it then cut it

2. Wash the vegetables and place it in a colander and dry it

3. While you're waiting, cut the garlic into small pieces

4. After that, add oil to your frying pan and turn the heat on

5. Add the garlic into the pan when the oil has been heated, this can bring out the smell of the garlic

*Reminder: Remember to keep on stirring to avoid them being burnt

6. After the garlic has a slight change of colour, started to add the vegetables and stir

7. Add a bit of salt at last for a bit of flavour

8. After the vege are soft enough you can put it onto your plate

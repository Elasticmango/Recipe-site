Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

Egg(s) x 2

Salt 1 tea spoon (Optional)

Milk 30 mL (Optional)

Steps:

1. Wash and crack the 2 eggs into a bowl (Tip 1)

2. Add the salt and milk, salt can help with the flavour

3. Beat the eggs throughly

4. Add oil to a pan, after that turn on the stove

5. When the oil is hot enough (usually wait 10 seconds) then pour the eggs into the pan

6. Push the eggs from the bottom, those has been cooked and should be solid

7. Turn off the heat when the egg is at 70%-80% solid

8. When the egg is fully in solid form, you can put it in a plate and enjoy :)
